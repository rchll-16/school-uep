Courses Programs

The University of Eastern Pangasinan (UEP) provides professional education in Elementary and Secondary Education to meet the growing demand for teachers. The core curriculum focuses on generalist teaching across different learning areas, while the intensive high school program focuses on specialized academic areas. UEP aims to produce educators with professional, ethical, and behavioral qualities, contributing to national development and education objectives.

The hospitality industry, including airlines, hotels, resorts, travel agencies, and tour operators, significantly impacts a country's living standards, generates income, and promotes cultural awareness. The UEP Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management prepares students for a career in the industry, providing practical skills in hotel, restaurant, and resort operations, as well as training in food and beverage service, housekeeping, event management, banquet and catering, and commercial cooking.

Business administration courses aim to prepare students for success in various fields, including finance, marketing, economics, accounting, statistics, problem-solving, human resources, and decision-making. These programs require critical thinking, problem-solving, and problem-solving skills, making math proficiency an asset. The University of Eastern Pangasinan offers a Bachelor Degree in Business, focusing on politics, ethics, sensitivity, innovativeness, creativity, and dynamics. Students can specialize in areas like Financial Management and Marketing Management, aiming to produce efficient and effective businesspeople.

The College of Information Technology (CIT) was established in 2007 at the University of Eastern Pangasinan to adapt to evolving trends in information and communications technology. CIT aims to create a vibrant learning community by offering a wide range of academic disciplines, up-to-date practices, and IT knowledge to equip students with the skills needed to adapt to a dynamic culture and contribute to society as qualified IT professionals.

The Criminology Program aims to provide professionally competent graduates capable of crime prevention, detection, law enforcement, and rehabilitation. It fosters leadership, integrity, camaraderie, accountability, and responsibility, while enhancing patriotism and service. The program provides scientific knowledge, enhances constitutional and human rights knowledge, and applies recent research in law enforcement, criminal investigation, and correctional administration. The goal is to create law-abiding citizens who embody integrity and trustworthiness.

The Philippines recognizes midwifery as a primary healthcare service, addressing basic health needs and ensuring patient safety. UEP focuses on forming professional midwives to meet changing healthcare demands and promote health. The Diploma in Midwifery program offers a two-year program, including clinical practicums in midwifery, normal obstetrics, high-risk obstetrics, infant care, family planning, and ethics. Graduates are qualified to take the government licensure examination.
